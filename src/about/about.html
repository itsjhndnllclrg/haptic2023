About HAPTIC™

“Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower.”

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a platform where the ultimate, cinematic experience can be shared by everyone. HAPTIC™ is a online and offline shopping destination, providing the most advanced devices at the best prices. HAPTIC™ provides an amazing solution to fulfill your dreams by offering an extraordinary range including Cellphones, Laptops/Netbook, Smartphones, Tablets and many more.

We believe that electronics should be fun, never a chore. We believe in the power of an unforgettable experience, coupled with an exceptional device or accessory. You deserve to be dazzled by a world of extraordinary devices and accessories, just like the stars in our sky. It's time to take charge of your tech.

Our Mission

HAPTIC™ was founded on the principle of innovation and product quality, creating a platform where customers can shop with confidence. We want to take shopping to an entirely new level. HAPTIC™ is built to be enjoyable and rewarding by providing extraordinary customer service, unmatched customer satisfaction and a customer experience that no other company can match.

HAPTIC™ is the all-in one shopping destination for you, where you can get premium devices and accessories for your needs. Our wide range of products are available in all kinds of brands, with unbeatable prices. You can browse through our online shop to find a device or accessory that suits your needs.

Our Objectives

  • To be the first and leading innovative device, accessories and tech brand.

  • To be the best online shop for innovative devices and accessories, so our users can get a wide range of products for their home, car, bedroom and more.

  • To close the gap of the digital divide.
